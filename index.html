A closer look

☕ At a café in Paris 🇫🇷 French

Ask if someone speaks English

Excusez-moi, vous parlez anglais ?

ex-koo-ZAY mwah, voo par-LAY ahn-GLEH

Cultural note Always start with "Excusez-moi". Many French people speak some English but appreciate you trying French first.

You might hear back

Oui, je parle un peu anglais. wee, zhuh PARL uhn PUH ahn-GLEH Yes, I speak a little English.
Désolé, je ne parle pas anglais. day-zoh-LAY, zhuh nuh PARL pah ahn-GLEH Sorry, I don't speak English.
Un peu, qu'est-ce que vous voulez ? uhn PUH, kess kuh voo voo-LAY A little — what do you need?

Questions

Good questions

When does SayItHere launch?

SayItHere is launching this week on the App Store for iPhone, pending Apple's final approval. It's free to download.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once your language is loaded, everything — phrases, pronunciation guides, audio playback — works completely offline. No data plan required abroad.

How is this different from Google Translate?

Google Translate is built for typing sentences and getting translations back. SayItHere is built for moments — it knows where you are and gives you curated, culturally-aware phrases you can use instantly. No typing, no awkward phrasing, no wondering what the response means.

Which languages are available?

French, Italian, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Turkish, Czech, Dutch, Greek, and Japanese — with more on the way.

Do I need an account?

No account, no sign-up, no email. Open the app, choose your language, and start speaking.

Is my data really private?

Completely. SayItHere works entirely on your device. Your location is never transmitted. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection — ever.
