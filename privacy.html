Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

SayItHere has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app makes two kinds of network requests: an anonymous location lookup to find nearby places (Live Mode only), and downloads of phrase content from GitHub to stay current. No personally identifiable information is included in either request. That's the extent of network activity.

Location data

SayItHere requests access to your device's location in order to detect nearby places (such as cafés, restaurants, and hotels) and suggest relevant phrases for that context in your chosen language.

  • Live Mode sends your approximate coordinates to the OpenStreetMap Overpass API to find nearby places — this is the only time your location leaves your device
  • No personally identifiable information is included in this request
  • Location is only accessed when you actively use Live Mode
  • We never store, log, or transmit a history of your locations
  • You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings

Phrase data updates

SayItHere downloads updated phrase content from GitHub to keep vocabulary current without requiring an app update. Only phrase files are downloaded — no user data, device information, or identifiers are sent during this process.

Third-party services

SayItHere connects to two third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap Overpass API — used by Live Mode to find places near your location. Your approximate coordinates are sent as part of this request. Subject to the OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub — used to download updated phrase content. No user data is sent. Subject to the GitHub Privacy Policy.

Neither service receives any personally identifiable information from SayItHere.

Data we do not collect

  • No names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No crash reporting sent off-device
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No purchase or payment information
  • No contacts, photos, or other personal content

Data stored on your device

SayItHere stores the following information locally on your device only, using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your phone:

  • Your saved favourite phrases
  • Your recently viewed scenarios
  • Your app settings (voice preference, speech speed)
  • A cached copy of phrase data for offline use

Children's privacy

SayItHere does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no forms, and no data submission of any kind.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Any changes will maintain the same commitment to minimal data practices.
